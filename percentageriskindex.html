Percentage Risk Index Calculator

Note:

Percentage Risk Index calculates the proportional contribution of a specific risk to the total risk profile, enabling prioritized risk management. It helps organizations identify high-impact risks and allocate resources effectively. By analyzing individual risk components, decision-makers can implement targeted mitigation strategies to enhance overall safety and efficiency.
