Lifting Force per Anchor at Building Site Calculator

Note:

The Lifting Force Formula calculates the force per anchor required to lift heavy slabs safely by accounting for total weight, cable angle, lifting load factor, and the number of anchors.

Parameters Explained:

  • G (Total Weight): The total weight of the slab in kN.
  • z (Cable Angle Factor): Accounts for the angle of cables during lifting.
  • f (Lifting Load Factor): Considers safety factors during lifting.
  • n (Number of Anchors): Distributes the load across multiple anchors.

Real-Life Applications:

  • Construction: Lifting heavy precast concrete slabs.
  • Industrial Handling: Transporting large machinery parts.

Conclusion:

Ensuring accurate lifting force calculations prevents structural failures and maintains workplace safety.
