NEMA and IP Ratings Calculator

Note:

NEMA and IP ratings are both used to measure the protection of electrical enclosures, but they differ in some ways.

  • NEMA Ratings: Used mainly in North America. They consider protection against water, solid objects, corrosion, gases, and hazardous environments.
  • IP Ratings: Used worldwide. They only focus on protection against water and solid objects.

This calculator is for educational purposes only. It helps compare NEMA and IP ratings but does not provide a direct conversion between them.
