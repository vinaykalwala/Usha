Army Body Fat Calculator

What Measurements Are Needed?

According to the US Army Regulation 600-9, the following measurements are required:

  • Height: Measure without shoes, standing straight.
  • Neck: Measure below the larynx (Adam's apple).
  • Waist: Measure at the navel level for men, and around the narrowest part of the abdomen for women.
  • Hips (for women only): Measure at the widest part of the buttocks or hips.
