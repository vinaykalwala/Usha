Parameters Explanation:

  • Steam Temperature (Tₛ in °C): The temperature of steam inside the heat exchanger.
  • Temperature Design Constant (TDC): A constant that defines the efficiency of heat exchange; must be greater than 1.
  • Secondary Fluid Outlet Temperature (T₂ in °C): The temperature at which the secondary fluid exits the heat exchanger.
  • Secondary Fluid Inlet Temperature (T₁ in °C): The calculated value that ensures the required heat exchange.
