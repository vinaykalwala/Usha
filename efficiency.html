Why Must Input Values Be Greater Than 0?

To ensure the calculation is meaningful, input values must be greater than zero:

  • Production (PCs) > 0: No production means no efficiency can be calculated.
  • SMV > 0: Every product takes some time to manufacture.
  • Manpower > 0: If no workers are available, no time can be utilized.
  • Working Time > 0: Without time allocation, efficiency becomes undefined.

Practical Applications

Efficiency measurement is widely used in:

  • Manufacturing: To assess production performance.
  • Quality Control: To identify inefficiencies and improve processes.
  • Safety & Environmental Monitoring: To ensure compliance with standards.

Conclusion

A **higher efficiency** value means **better productivity and cost savings**. By tracking efficiency, companies can improve workflow, reduce waste, and maximize output.
