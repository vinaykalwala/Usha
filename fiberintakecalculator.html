Fiber Intake Calculator

What is Fiber, and Why is it Important?

Fiber is a dietary nutrient found in plant-based foods that aids in digestion, helps maintain bowel health, and can reduce the risk of heart disease and colorectal cancer. It is essential for a healthy diet and comes in two forms: soluble and insoluble.

How is Fiber Intake Calculated?

The calculator uses your daily calorie intake to determine your recommended fiber intake. The general guideline is to consume 14 grams of fiber for every 1,000 calories you consume per day.

Foods High in Fiber

Recommended Daily Fiber Intake:

  • Women: 25 grams per day
  • Men: 38 grams per day

To meet your daily fiber needs, consider adding these fiber-rich foods to your diet:

Fruits
Apples, bananas, berries, pears, oranges.
Vegetables
Broccoli, carrots, spinach, sweet potatoes.
Legumes
Lentils, chickpeas, black beans, peas.
Nuts and Seeds
Almonds, chia seeds, flaxseeds, sunflower seeds.
Whole Grains
Oats, quinoa, brown rice, whole wheat bread.
