Cost of Effluent Saved by Returning Condensate Calculator

This calculator helps industries estimate the cost savings from returning condensate, reducing effluent discharge expenses, and promoting sustainable resource management. By minimizing effluent wastage, industries can cut costs and enhance environmental responsibility.

Understanding the Parameters:

  • Expected Improvement in Condensate Return (%): The percentage increase in the amount of condensate returned to the boiler system.
  • Average Boiler Evaporation Rate (kg/h): The amount of steam the boiler generates per hour.
  • Operational Hours per Year (h/year): The total hours the boiler operates annually.
  • Cost of Effluent per m³ (₹): The price of effluent discharge per cubic meter.

What the Result Means:

The calculated value represents the estimated cost savings per year achieved by improving condensate return. Higher savings indicate better efficiency and reduced effluent expenses.

Environmental Impact & ISO 14001 Standards:

By optimizing condensate return, industries can significantly reduce effluent discharge and costs. This practice aligns with ISO 14001, an international environmental management standard focused on sustainable resource use and reducing industrial environmental impacts.
