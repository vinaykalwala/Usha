Sleep Calculator

What Are Sleep Cycles?

Sleep consists of 90-minute-long cycles, repeated throughout the night. Each cycle includes stages of light sleep, deep sleep, and REM (rapid eye movement) sleep. Waking up at the end of a cycle helps you feel refreshed and alert.

Why Are Sleep Cycles Important?

Waking up in the middle of a sleep cycle can leave you feeling groggy and tired. By aligning your wake-up time with the end of a cycle, you maximize the benefits of sleep and improve your overall well-being.

How to Use the Sleep Calculator

Follow these steps to find your ideal bedtime:

  1. Enter Time to Fall Asleep: How long it usually takes you to fall asleep (default is 15 minutes).
  2. Set Target Wake-Up Time: Choose the time you want to wake up.
  3. Custom Sleep Cycles (Optional): If your sleep cycles are not 90 minutes, enable the custom option and enter your cycle lengths.
  4. Calculate: Click "Calculate Sleep Times" to see the best bedtimes for 1 to 6 sleep cycles.

Recommended Sleep Durations

The National Sleep Foundation recommends the following sleep durations based on age:

  • Adults (18-64 years): 7-9 hours
  • Older Adults (65+ years): 7-8 hours
  • Teenagers (14-17 years): 8-10 hours
  • Children (6-13 years): 9-11 hours
