Directional Survey Calculator

Note:

The Directional Survey Calculator helps determine the horizontal displacement (D) of a wellbore during drilling operations. Accurate directional surveys are essential for efficient well placement, collision avoidance, and maximizing reservoir contact.

Parameters Explanation

  • Displacement (D): The horizontal distance the wellbore has deviated from the surface location.
  • Measured Depth (MD): The total length of the wellbore measured along its path.
  • Inclination (I): The angle of deviation from the vertical.

By entering the required values and clicking Calculate Displacement, the tool will determine how far the well has moved horizontally.

Quality & ISO Compliance:

  • ISO 9001 (Quality Management): Ensures accuracy in directional surveys to meet drilling specifications.
  • ISO 14001 (Environmental Management): Prevents unintended environmental impact by optimizing well trajectories.
  • ISO 45001 (Occupational Health & Safety): Enhances drilling safety by minimizing risks of well collisions.
