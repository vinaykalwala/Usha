Mass of Waste Activated Sludge Calculator

Note:

This calculator determines the Mass of Waste Activated Sludge, which is crucial for assessing sludge management in biological wastewater treatment systems.

It is widely used in wastewater treatment, environmental engineering, and process optimization to ensure proper sludge management and system stability.

Explanation of Parameters:

  • Maximum Yield Coefficient (Y): Ratio of biomass produced per unit of substrate consumed.
  • Total Influent Flow Rate (Q): The flow rate entering the treatment system.
  • Influent Substrate Concentration (So): Concentration of organic matter in the influent wastewater.
  • Effluent Substrate Concentration (Se): Concentration of organic matter in the treated effluent.
  • Endogenous Decay Rate (kd): Rate at which microorganisms consume their own biomass for energy.
  • MLSS Concentration in Reactor (X): Mixed Liquor Suspended Solids concentration in the aeration tank.
  • Reactor Volume (V): Total volume of the biological treatment reactor.
