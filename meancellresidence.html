Mean Cell Residence Sludge Retention Time (SRT) Calculator

Note:

This calculator determines the Sludge Retention Time (SRT), which is crucial for assessing the efficiency of biological wastewater treatment systems.

It is widely used in wastewater treatment, environmental engineering, and process optimization to ensure proper sludge management and system stability.

Explanation of Parameters:

  • Reactor Volume (V): The total volume of the biological treatment reactor.
  • MLSS Concentration in Reactor (X): The concentration of microbial biomass in the aeration tank.
  • Waste Activated Sludge Rate (Qw): The rate at which excess sludge is removed from the system.
  • MLSS Concentration in Return Sludge (Xr): The microbial concentration in the returned activated sludge.
  • MLSS Concentration in Effluent (Xe): The concentration of suspended solids in the treated effluent.
  • Total Influent Flow Rate (Q): The overall flow rate entering the treatment system.
