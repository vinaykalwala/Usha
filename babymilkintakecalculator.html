Baby Milk Intake Calculator

Note:

This calculator helps estimate your baby's total daily milk intake and milk required per feeding based on three important inputs: age group, weight, and number of feedings per day. These are only general recommendations—please always consult your pediatrician for personalized guidance.

📌 Explanation of Parameters:

  • Baby's Age Group: The estimated daily milk requirement depends on your baby's age bracket. Younger infants typically need more milk per kilogram of body weight.
  • Baby's Weight (in kg): Milk intake is calculated based on your baby's body weight using age-based ml/kg/day values.
  • Number of Feedings per Day: This divides the total daily milk into equal portions for each feeding session.

📊 Age-Based Equivalence Table:

Age (Months) Milk Requirement (ml/kg/day)

✅ Conclusion:

The result provided includes two key pieces of information:

  • Total Daily Milk Requirement: The estimated total amount of milk your baby may need over a 24-hour period.
  • Milk Per Feeding: The amount of milk to give during each feeding, based on the number of feedings you entered.
Use these estimates to monitor feeding routines and ensure your baby receives adequate nutrition. Remember, every baby is different, and growth patterns vary. Consult your doctor if you have any concerns. 💙👶🍼
