Note:

The Incidence Rate Calculator helps measure workplace safety by calculating the number of injuries per 1,000 employees.

Enter the total number of workplace injuries and the average number of employees to calculate the incidence rate.

A result greater than 1,000 indicates that the number of injuries is higher than the average number of employees, which can happen if injuries are frequent relative to the workforce size. This is mathematically valid and reflects the rate's proportionality to 1,000 employees.

A lower incidence rate indicates fewer injuries and better workplace safety. Use this metric to evaluate and improve workplace safety conditions.
