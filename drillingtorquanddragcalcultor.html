Drilling Torque & Drag Calculator

Note:

The Drilling Torque & Drag Calculator helps determine the torque experienced by a drill string during drilling operations. Torque is a critical factor in drill string design, as it affects the efficiency and safety of drilling operations.

Parameters Explanation

  • Torque (T): The rotational force experienced by the drill string, measured in foot-pounds-force (ft-lbf).
  • Force Applied (F): The axial force applied to the drill string, measured in pounds-force (lbf).
  • Radius (r): The distance from the center of rotation to the point where the force is applied, measured in feet.

By entering the required values and clicking Calculate Torque, the tool will determine the torque and provide feedback on the drilling conditions.
