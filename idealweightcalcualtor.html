Ideal Weight Calculator

What is Ideal Weight?

The ideal weight represents a healthy body mass based on various formulas. It is an estimate of the weight range that is considered healthy for a person of a given height and gender. Maintaining an ideal weight is important for overall health and can reduce the risk of chronic diseases such as diabetes, heart disease, and hypertension.

Formulas Used:

  • Peterson Formula: A newer formula that takes into account height and gender to estimate ideal weight.
  • Miller Formula: Based on height, this formula provides an estimate of ideal weight for both men and women.
  • Robinson Formula: A modification of the Devine formula, it is widely used in medical settings.
  • Devine Formula: One of the most commonly used formulas, originally developed for medical dosage calculations.
  • Hamwi Formula: Similar to the Devine formula, it is often used in clinical practice.
  • Broca Formula: A simple formula that subtracts a fixed number from height to estimate ideal weight.
  • Lorentz Formula: Adjusts ideal weight based on gender and height, providing a more tailored estimate.

Healthy BMI Range:

The Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. A healthy BMI range is typically between 18.5 and 24.9. This calculator also provides the weight range corresponding to a healthy BMI for your height.
